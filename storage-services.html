Boat Bottom Painting Service

If your boat stays in the water year round or during the boating season; putting bottom paint on is a necessity. You need to protect your hull from weeds, barnacles, and other aquatic organisms from attaching themselves to the underwater portion of your boat’s hull. During storage we can apply bottom paint to your boats hull.
