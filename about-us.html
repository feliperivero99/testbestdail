About M22 Detailing

Founded in Leland, Michigan in June of 2017. M22 Detailing strives to provide the highest quality detailing and concierge services to our customers.

Cars and boats are our passion and we would love you make yours look new again. We are a team of the most experienced detailers in Northern Michigan and we have a portfolio of cars under our belt ranging from the 1930s to 2020.

We offer a full range of services: ceramic coatings, interior and exterior detailing, wax and polishing, paint correction, and concierge.

We are proud to be one of the only Ceramic Pro Certified Installers in the area. We carry liability and garage-keepers insurance to ensure your vehicle and/or boat is safe with us.

Allow us to make your prized possession flawless. Call us today @ 231-409-5864.
